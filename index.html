Local History & Heritage Education

Learn about your local culture and heritage.

Heritage Places of Karnataka

Karnataka is rich in cultural heritage with numerous historical sites and monuments. Some notable heritage places include:

  • Hampi: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire.
  • Mysore Palace: A magnificent palace and a major tourist attraction in Mysore.
  • Badami Caves: Rock-cut cave temples famous for their architecture and sculptures.
  • Belur and Halebidu: Renowned for their exquisite Hoysala architecture.
  • Sravanabelagola: Famous for the giant monolithic statue of Gomateshwara Bahubali.

Other Heritage Places

India and the world have many other heritage sites worth exploring:

  • Taj Mahal: Iconic white marble mausoleum in Agra, India.
  • Ajanta and Ellora Caves: Ancient rock-cut caves with beautiful murals and sculptures.
  • Khajuraho Temples: Famous for their intricate erotic sculptures.
  • Great Wall of China: Historic fortification stretching across northern China.
  • Machu Picchu: Ancient Incan city located in Peru.
